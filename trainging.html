Trainings and Exposure Visits

Advanced Regional Leadership Exchange program (ARLEP), Uva Province, Sri Lanka

ARLEP on Local Administration and Good Governance is a leadership exchange program MCMI organize in collaboration with AIT extension annually for regional local government administrative leaders. This program aims to provide opportunities to local administrative leaders to meet regional leaders in similar administrative setup and share experiences.

  • In 2011 successfully conducted ARLEP regional leadership program in Vietnam. The program aimed was to share experiences on integrated rural and urban development projects , Agro-Based technology transfer and tourism industry. The team visited 5 provinces in Vietnam.
  • In 2012, ARLEP program conducted in North of Thailand city of Chiang Mai. The program focused on integrated Agricultural Development.
  • In 2013, Advance Regional Leadership Exchange Program (ARLEP) conducted in two countries Thailand and Indonesia. The program focused to explore, participate facilitate and manage provincial council administrative functions and provided opportunity to interact with local administrative officials in Thailand and Indonesia to exchange their experiences on education, tourism, training and state land administration in local government level in Thailand and Indonesia.

Implementation, Operation and Maintenance of Flood Control Drainage and Irrigation in Sri Lanka for Bangladesh Water Development Board

Since 2012, MCMI in collaboration with AIT Extension has been organizing two weeks capacity development training and exposure visit program on “Implementation, Operation & Maintenance (O&M), Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E) for Flood Control, Drainage and Irrigation” in Sri Lanka for Bangladesh Water Development Board and the fourth batch completed its visit in May 2014. Forty eight participants have been trained under this project in Sri Lanka. Multiple Challenge Management International (MCMI) in Sri Lanka with direction of AIT Extension, Thailand organize this programs and coordinated with the Ministry of Irrigation & Water Resources Management, Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and Development Cooperation, Urban Development Authority, Mahaweli Authority of Sri Lanka and other responsible flood controlled, drainage and irrigation related institutions and civil engineering departments in national and provincial level in Sri Lanka. These institutions provided expert resource persons to the program and facilitate to arrange study visits.

Management and Maintenance of Road Networks in USA - Ministry of Local Government and Provincial Council's, Sri Lanka

During July 2014, MCMI organized one week international exposure visit program on Management and maintenance of road networks in USA. This program enhanced knowledge and skills of senior executives and policy makers in local government and provincial councils on techniques and best practices on management and maintenance of the road networks in USA. This program mainly emphasis on preparing a strategy for provincial roads management. The program designed to allow maximum on-site exposure in New Jersey, Buffalo, Washington DC and New York cities. This visit provided opportunities for participants to interact with experts on the provincial roads in the 4 cities and, share experiences, discuss issues relevant to the management and maintenance of road networks and preparing strategies for management of provincial roads more efficiently and in higher standards.
